SPORTS AND ENTERTAINMENT TECHNOLOGY ALLIANCE

The mission of the Sports and Entertainment Technology Alliance is to foster education on current and new technologies, and promote best practices for safer and smarter schools, sport and entertainment venues and events.

The Alliance is the driving force for identifying innovative safety and security solutions through integrated technology design, development, and process enhancement with respect to sport venue operations management and associated first responder capabilities.

Given the exponential change in technology, the establishment of the Alliance is critical. Integrated solutions and technology continue to serve a major role in sport venue operations management and associated safety/security functions. Audience

The University of Southern Mississippi welcomes companies interested in advancing sport and entertainment safety and security technology to consider joining the Sports and Entertainment Technology Alliance.
